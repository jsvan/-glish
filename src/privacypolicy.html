-Glish Privacy Policy

What I use and collect


Nothing

I do not receive or store any information from you, not your identity, browsing history, or page information. The app works completely locally except for two image requests from its github project location. Your usage of this app is completely private with no tracking at all.

Purpose of Chrome Storage

I use your browser's chrome sync storage to store your language choice and vocabulary information.

Purpose of Tabs

My app peeks at your url to see if it's blacklisted, to not run the app. This URL is not sent anywhere external or is used/collected in any other way.
