-glish












    You selected a 5% chance to translate words from a vocabulary list with 0 unique words in it.

Features and Tricks

    ★ HOVER on a word  to reveal its original English word.

    ★ CLICK on a word  to rotate through matching translated words.

    ★ CLICK+HOLD on a word  to access its detailed information page through Wiktionary.


    ★ If it bugs out, refresh the page or jiggle some menu options. Sorry!

About

    The dictionary uses the first 4000 most common words, in order of frequency. As you progress through the dictionary, rarer words are encountered.

    This version uses a unigram langauge model for greedy translations.

    Future versions could include n-gram models or in browser neural models if the performance is good enough.
